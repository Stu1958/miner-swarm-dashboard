🖥️ Why a Dedicated Dashboard?

Instant Fleet View See instantly that all 8 miners are working without touching your phone or PC.
Always-On Monitoring Lives on your desk as a standalone hardware monitor—no logins required.
Multi-Room Support Set one up in every room to monitor the swarm from anywhere in the house.
Zero Latency Real-time updates delivered directly from your miners to the display.

⚡ Instant Web Installer

Connect your ESP32-CYD via USB. Hold BOOT, tap RESET, then click below to flash.

📏 App Setup Naming (8 Chars Max)

Use these rules in the app setup to enable custom hardware logic. Case-insensitive.

Avalon 3S: Use NANO & 3S
Avalon 3: Use NANO & 3
N-Series: MUST start with NX or NQ
Bitaxe/Other: Any other name (e.g. RIG_01)
